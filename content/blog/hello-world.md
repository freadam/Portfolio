# Welcome to My Blog

Hello! I'm **Freadam Abebe**, a game developer and designer based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. This blog is where I'll share my thoughts on game development, design challenges, and lessons learned along the way.

## What to Expect

- **Dev Logs** — Behind-the-scenes looks at my professional and personal game projects
- **Design Deep Dives** — Explorations of game mechanics, board game design, and interactive experiences
- **Tutorials & Tips** — Practical guides on Unity, Godot, and game development workflows

## A Bit About My Work

I've spent years building games at [Qene Games](https://qenetech.com/), bringing traditional Ethiopian board games like *Mancala Adventures*, *Dama Negest*, and *Silet* to mobile platforms. Now I'm exploring new frontiers in both digital and analog game design.
